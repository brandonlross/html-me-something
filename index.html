Birth

I was born in August 19, 1993 in St. Louis, MO. I am the oldest of three, one brother (Broderick) and one sister (Brittany). My parents are Louis and Sherry Ross. I have lived in St. Louis my whole life, more specifically in the North County area. We have always had dogs in our house growing up - miniature pinschers & pitbulls. My father also keeps fish in his two fish tanks.

Early Life

My early life...hmmm

My early life was very centered around sports which kind of remains true to this day. I attended Grannemann elementary school until 7th grade, when I switched schools to Salem Lutheran Elementary for my middle school years. I began basketball at the age of 3 and football at the age of 7 which I continued through college. I played youth football for the Florissant Raiders and AAU basketball for the St. Louis Hawks, Xplosion and Majestics.

High School

Beginning in the fall of 2007 I started attending Lutheran North High School, where I participated in football, basketball and track. Overall, I would say that I enjoyed my time in high school. I met a lot of new people and experienced new things. I feel it really prepared me well for the college work. My basketball team lost the state championship my freshman year and our football team made the playoffs every year I was there. I was an all-conference linebacker and all-region as well as a senior. My junior year I started at quarterback.

I graduated from Lutheran North High School in spring of 2011.

College

Wild Times in the Chuck

In the fall of 2011 I started at Eastern Illinois University, located in Charleston, IL aka "The Chuck". This was probably the most fun time of my life to this point. I met so many different people and had so many memorable experiences. During college, I participated on the football team and won 2 conference championships. I also received the scholar athlete award. I became a member of Kappa Alpha Psi fraternity inc. I majored in business management and management information systems. I graduated in December of 2015.
